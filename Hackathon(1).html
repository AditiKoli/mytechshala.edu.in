About Hackathon

Vidyalanakar Polytechnic's Hackathon is a initiative to provide students with a platform to solve some of the pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem-solving.

Objectives

  • To create functioning software or hardware by the end of the event.

  • It include the programming language used, the operating system, an application, an API, or the subject and the demographic group of the programmers.

  • To provide students an understanding of the expectations of industry.

    •

    How to form a team for Hackathon?

  • Each team would comprise of 6 members including the team leader.

  • It is compulsory to have at least a girl member in a Team.

  • Repetition of member in 2 different teams is not allowed.

  • Each team has to select one faculty as their team mentor who will be with team through out the competition.

  • Once team have registered, changes in team members or mentor will not be encouraged.
